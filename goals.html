My personal goals


To bridge the gap between business and technology. During my internship at Bank of America, I utilized Natural Language Processing to investigate which aspects of consumer spending the enterprise can improve. Ultimately, I want to help business better understand and use technology while guiding technology to be business-oriented.

To deliver quality, ethical, and consumer-oriented products. Interning on the Docs team at Microsoft taught me to always see the products from the consumers' perspective. My goal is combining ethical practices with consumer-based decision making to develop functional, sustainable, and inclusive software.

To inspire young girls to pursue a career in technology and create a welcoming environment for women in tech. I have led my University's ACM-W chapter, where I hosted workshops and social events for the women in tech community. In the future, I hope to establish educational opportunities for students in Vietnam aspiring to enter the tech industry.
